Our Commitment

Indianeers’ Commitment to CSR

At Indianeers Media Private Limited, our CSR initiatives focus on empowering communities through food safety education, skill development, and health awareness. We aim to create sustainable impact by equipping individuals with knowledge and opportunities to thrive.

Community Food Safety Workshops

We conduct free FoSTaC-based workshops in rural and urban areas, teaching food safety practices to vendors, homemakers, and small businesses. Over 10,000 individuals have benefited, improving community health standards.

Skill Training for Underprivileged Youth

In partnership with NSDC, we provide skill training in food safety and hospitality to underprivileged youth. Our programs have empowered 5,000+ individuals with jobs and entrepreneurial opportunities.

Health and Hygiene Campaigns

Our campaigns in schools and slums promote hygiene and safe food handling, reaching 15,000+ people. We distribute educational materials and conduct interactive sessions to drive awareness.
