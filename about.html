About Us

Empowering India with Food Safety and Skills

Indianeers Media Private Limited is a leading provider of FSSAI-approved Food Safety Training and Certification (FoSTaC) programs. Since 2012, we have trained over 30,000 individuals across 23 states, fostering compliance and safety in the food industry.

Founded in 2012, Indianeers began with a mission to empower Indian youth through skill development. Partnering with NSDC, we expanded to over 450 training centers, certifying over 10,000 food safety supervisors through FoSTaC.

Today, we are a trusted partner for FSSAI compliance, transforming the food industry nationwide.

Our mission is to deliver accessible, high-quality FoSTaC training to every food business in India, ensuring FSSAI compliance and promoting a culture of food safety.

We aim to equip supervisors with the skills to protect consumers and elevate industry standards.

We envision a future where every food business in India operates with the highest safety and hygiene standards, driven by well-trained professionals.

Indianeers strives to lead the transformation of food safety practices across the nation.
