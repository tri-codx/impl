Introduction

About Indianeers Media Private Limited

Foundation

Established in 2012, Indianeers Media Private Limited began as a skill development organization with a vision to empower Indian youth. Partnering with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), we launched vocational training programs in key sectors like IT, agriculture, and hospitality, starting with 50 training centers across 5 states.

Progression

Over the past decade, Indianeers has scaled to over 450 training centers across 23 states, training more than 30,000 candidates and facilitating employment for over 22,000 individuals. Our partnerships with Sector Skill Councils, government initiatives like PMKVY, and industry leaders have solidified our role as a leader in skill development, including specialized programs like FoSTaC.

About Us

Empowering the Food Industry with FoSTaC Training

Indianeers Media Private Limited is a leading provider of FSSAI-approved Food Safety Training and Certification (FoSTaC) programs across India. We empower food businesses, from street vendors to large enterprises, to achieve compliance and ensure consumer safety.

Founded with a passion for food safety, Indianeers began as a mission to bridge the gap in standardized training for food businesses in India. Our journey started with FoSTaC, empowering thousands of food safety supervisors nationwide.

Today, we are a trusted partner for FSSAI compliance, delivering high-quality training that transforms the food industry.

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality FoSTaC training to every food business in India, ensuring compliance with FSSAI standards and promoting a culture of food safety.

We strive to equip food safety supervisors with the knowledge and skills to protect consumers and elevate industry standards.

We envision a future where every food business in India operates with the highest standards of safety and hygiene, driven by well-trained professionals certified through FoSTaC.

Indianeers aims to be the leading force in transforming food safety practices nationwide.
